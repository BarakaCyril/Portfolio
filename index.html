The Artist

I've never waited for the “right way” to make something — I just start. Sometimes with code. Sometimes with a sketch. Sometimes with a strange idea and a mop I probably shouldn't be touching.

My background blends traditional art with a love for technology — the kind of mix that lets me build things that feel crafted, not manufactured.

Whether I'm designing a visual identity, prototyping a UI, or editing a clip just to see where it goes, I treat every piece of work like a question worth exploring.

I believe creativity doesn't have to be clean. It just has to be honest.

My Philosophy

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works and how it makes people feel."
