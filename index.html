The Artist

I'm a digital artist who believes that every pixel has a story to tell. With a background in traditional arts and a passion for modern technology, I create experiences that bridge the gap between the tangible and the digital.

My journey began with oil paints and canvas, but the digital realm opened up infinite possibilities. Today, I craft interfaces, illustrations, and experiences that carry the warmth of hand-painted art into the digital age.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me sketching in coffee shops, exploring museums, or experimenting with new creative techniques that push the boundaries of what's possible.
